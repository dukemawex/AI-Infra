NGO FIELD REPORT - HORN OF AFRICA HUMANITARIAN INFRASTRUCTURE REVIEW
Report ID: RPT-005
Date: 2023-11-15
Organization: Infrastructure for Humanity (IFH)
Author: Regional Director Fatima Al-Rashid

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
=================
Emergency and development infrastructure review across Ethiopia (Addis Ababa
and Oromia region) and Djibouti. Context: Post-drought recovery and
conflict-affected area infrastructure restoration.

SITE ASSESSMENTS
================

Site 1: Addis Ababa Bole Sub-City Borehole (ASSET-0045)
Location: Addis Ababa, 9.0192, 38.7525
Type: Borehole
Depth: 110 meters
Current Status: operational
Installation Date: 2013-05-17
Last Maintenance: 2023-09-05
Condition Notes: Deep borehole serving dense urban community. Water
table depth has increased 8 meters over past decade due to over-extraction.
Current yield: 5.1 m³/hour (down from 7.2 m³/hour at installation).
Aquifer recharge monitoring recommended. System structurally sound.

Site 2: Oromia Region Solar Irrigation (ASSET-0078)
Location: Adama, 8.5400, 39.2700
Type: Solar System
Capacity: 28.5 kW
Current Status: operational
Installation Date: 2021-03-10
Last Maintenance: 2023-07-20
Condition Notes: Climate-smart agriculture program asset. Powers
irrigation pumps for 120 smallholder farmers. System performing at
design specifications. Remote monitoring active via satellite link.
This is a 2021 installation still under manufacturer warranty.

Site 3: Djibouti City Emergency Water Point (ASSET-0129)
Location: Djibouti City, 11.5886, 43.1451
Type: Borehole
Depth: 78 meters
Current Status: critical
Installation Date: 2010-08-25
Last Maintenance: 2022-01-30
Condition Notes: High-priority emergency asset serving refugee reception
center and surrounding community (~5,000 people). Pump motor has failed
twice in 2023. Significant corrosion in pump column due to high salinity
water. Temporary repairs holding but unreliable. Emergency replacement
authorized - awaiting procurement. No alternative water source within
reasonable distance.

Site 4: Hargeisa Solar Health Post (ASSET-0166)
Location: Hargeisa, 9.5600, 44.0650
Type: Solar System
Capacity: 6.5 kW
Current Status: degraded
Installation Date: 2017-11-30
Last Maintenance: 2023-03-15
Condition Notes: Health post solar system for vaccine refrigeration and
lighting. System is degraded due to battery capacity loss (45% of original
capacity). Risk of vaccine cold-chain failure. Battery replacement is
critical health priority. Cost: USD 4,200. Should be treated as medical
infrastructure - urgent.

HUMANITARIAN RISK ASSESSMENT
============================
Of assessed sites in conflict-affected areas:
- 40% show deferred maintenance patterns
- Average time since last maintenance: 18 months
- Critical infrastructure serving vulnerable populations: 6 sites
- Population at risk from infrastructure failure: ~28,000 people

POLICY RECOMMENDATION
=====================
Humanitarian infrastructure should be classified separately in asset
management systems with shorter maintenance intervals (6-month max)
and emergency repair pre-authorization under USD 5,000.

This report has been shared with OCHA, UNHCR regional offices, and
respective government infrastructure ministries.
