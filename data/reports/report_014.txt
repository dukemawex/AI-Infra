EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Field assessment mission to Minna and surrounding LGAs in Niger State. 4 infrastructure assets inspected over 6 days. Mission focus: water access and energy infrastructure serving rural and peri-urban communities.

SITE ASSESSMENTS
================

Site 1: Hand-pump borehole (installed 2016) [ID: 965AE046…]
Location: GPS: 8.0503°N, 14.2944°E — Minna
Asset Type: Borehole
Install Date: 2016-12-04
Last Maintenance: 2024-01-08
Current Status: DEGRADED
Usage Level: Medium

Issue Description:
  Pump stroke output reduced by approximately 30–40% compared to installation baseline. Leakage detected at pump head seal. Community of ~326 people partially relying on unsafe surface sources.

Maintenance History:
  Last maintenance was over 18 months ago. Recommend full pump overhaul within 60 days.

Current Status: NON-FUNCTIONAL
Usage Level: High
