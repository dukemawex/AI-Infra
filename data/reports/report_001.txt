Date: 2023-08-14
Organization: WaterAid East Africa

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
=================
This report documents field assessment of borehole infrastructure in the Nairobi
and Kisumu regions conducted between July 28 and August 10, 2023.

SITE ASSESSMENTS
================

Site 1: Kibera Community Borehole (ASSET-0023)
Location: Nairobi, -1.3133, 36.7897
Type: Borehole
Depth: 65 meters
Current Status: operational
Population Served: ~2,400 residents
Last Maintenance: 2023-03-15
Condition Notes: Pump motor showing early signs of wear. Recommend inspection
within 90 days. Water quality tests passed. Flow rate at 85% capacity.

Site 2: Kisumu North Solar Pump (ASSET-0087)
Location: Kisumu, -0.0917, 34.7680
Type: Solar System
Capacity: 15.5 kW
Current Status: degraded
Last Maintenance: 2022-11-20
Condition Notes: Two solar panels are cracked due to hailstorm damage in April
2023. Battery storage efficiency has dropped to 60%. Urgent panel replacement
required. Estimated cost: USD 3,200.

Site 3: Mathare Valley Borehole (ASSET-0041)
Location: Nairobi, -1.2595, 36.8572
Type: Borehole
Depth: 48 meters
Current Status: critical
Last Maintenance: 2021-06-10
Condition Notes: Pump has failed intermittently 3 times in the past 60 days.
Sand contamination detected in water samples. Immediate intervention required.
Community has resorted to using unsafe surface water sources.

RECOMMENDATIONS
===============
1. ASSET-0041 requires emergency maintenance within 30 days.
2. ASSET-0087 panel replacement should be scheduled within 60 days.
3. ASSET-0023 should be monitored and inspected in Q4 2023.

Budget Required: USD 18,500 (emergency + preventive maintenance)
